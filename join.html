Join the Lab

We’re recruiting motivated students and collaborators committed to health equity and community impact.

Students

  • Graduate (MS/PhD): Interests in physical activity, nutrition, sleep, child health, or community-based research.
  • Undergraduate RAs: Literature reviews, data collection, analysis, and outreach.

How to apply

  1. Send a single PDF to nan.zeng@umb.edu with: CV, brief statement (≤1 page), and 1–2 representative works.
  2. Indicate availability (hours/semester) and interests (e.g., exergaming, 24‑hr movement behaviors, community partnerships).

We value diversity, inclusion, and community-engaged scholarship.
