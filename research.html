Research

Our projects span physical activity promotion, sedentary behavior reduction, nutrition support, and integrated 24-hour movement approaches—co-designed with communities to advance health equity.

Ongoing

ALL-PLAY: School-Based Exergaming to Promote 24-Hour Movement

Design: Cluster randomized trial • Setting: Preschool • Population: 3–5 years

Co-designed exergaming + classroom movement breaks to increase daily activity, reduce sedentary time, and support sleep routines, with teacher training and family take-home kits.

SCREEN-SMART: Family Coaching to Reduce Sedentary Time

Design: Pragmatic RCT • Setting: Home & Head Start • Population: Families with preschoolers

Family-centered coaching and media plans to shift screen habits, increase light activity, and support healthier snacking routines.
