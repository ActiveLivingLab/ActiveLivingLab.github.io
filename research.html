Research

Reverse‑engineering behavior change in the real world—through rigorous trials, community partnerships, and open science.

Interventions

School‑based & community programs

Designing and evaluating interventions that promote PA, healthy eating, and sleep in preschoolers and families.

Tech‑enabled

Exergaming & VR approaches

Leveraging interactive technologies (e.g., exergames, VR) to enhance engagement and outcomes.

Measurement

24‑hour movement & dietary outcomes

Wearables, validated questionnaires, and mixed‑methods to capture activity, sedentary time, sleep, and diet.

Open science & data

Where possible, we share de‑identified code and materials to accelerate cumulative science and community impact.
