Health Equity • Community • Behavior Change

Active Living Lab (ALL)

We design, test, and scale evidence-based strategies to promote physical activity and health — especially among underserved populations. Our work integrates rigorous science with real-world partnerships across schools, families, and communities.

Join the lab Contact us

Mission

Promoting sustainable healthy lifestyles

ALL advances health equity via community-engaged research, education, and partnerships to support active, healthy living for children, families, and communities.

Focus Areas

Physical activity and health

We study movement behaviors and implement behavior change programs across school, family, and community settings.

Director

Dr. Nan Zeng

Assistant Professor, Department of Exercise & Health Sciences, UMass Boston. Research interests: physical activity promotion, exergaming, community-based interventions, and child health.

Recent highlights

Publication

Systematic review: 24-hour movement behaviors & cognition in preschoolers

Across 22 RCTs, cognitively engaging movement interventions yielded the largest cognitive benefits, emphasizing the importance of conducting integrated trials that address all movement behaviors.
