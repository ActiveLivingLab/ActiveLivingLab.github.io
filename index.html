Active Living Lab (ALL)

The Active Living Lab (ALL) is an independent research lab directed by Dr. Nan Zeng and housed in the Department of Exercise & Health Sciences at the University of Massachusetts Boston.

We study movement behaviors and design, test, and scale evidence-based strategies to promote physical activity and health — especially among families with young children in underserved communities.

Our work integrates rigorous science with real-world partnerships across schools, families, and communities.
