Active Living Lab (ALL)

We design, test, and scale evidence-based strategies to promote physical activity, nutrition, sleep, and overall well-being—especially in underserved communities. Our work blends rigorous science with real-world partnerships in schools, Head Start centers, and community programs.

Join the lab Read our papers

Mission

Promoting sustainable healthy lifestyles

ALL advances health equity via community-engaged research, education, and partnerships to support active, healthy living for children, families, and communities.

Focus Areas

Physical activity • Nutrition • Sleep

We study 24‑hour movement behaviors, dietary habits, and behavior-change strategies—from preschool settings to family and community interventions.

Director

Assistant Professor, Department of Exercise & Health Sciences, UMass Boston. Research interests: physical activity promotion, exergaming, community-based interventions, and child health.
